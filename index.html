EVERYTHING YOUR AI AGENT NEEDS
One platform to build, deploy, and manage all your intelligent agents.
📞

Voice Calling Agent

Deploy AI agents that make and receive calls just like a human. Handle thousands of calls simultaneously.

  • Upload contact lists via CSV or API
  • Natural language voice conversations
  • Real-time transcription & recording
  • Hindi, English & 50+ languages
  • Inbound + Outbound calling
📝

Script Manager

Write custom conversation scripts for your agents. Control exactly what your agent says.

  • Visual drag-and-drop script builder
  • Conditional branching & logic
  • Dynamic variable injection
  • A/B test different scripts
  • Pre-built templates for sales & support
📅

Auto Appointment Booking

Your AI agent automatically books, confirms, and reminds customers. Zero manual effort.

  • Google Calendar & Outlook sync
  • Smart slot availability detection
  • Automated SMS & email reminders
  • Reschedule & cancellation handling
  • CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot)

Chatbot Agent

Embed a powerful AI chatbot on your website. Train it on your data for 24/7 instant responses.

  • Train on PDFs, docs & websites
  • Embed on any website in 2 minutes
  • WhatsApp, Telegram & Slack integration
  • Human handoff when needed
  • Full conversation analytics
